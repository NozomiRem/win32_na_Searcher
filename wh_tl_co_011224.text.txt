text02 - アドバンスクエストでは倒したエネミーが<br>「<yellow>輝石<c>」と呼ばれるアイテムをドロップする場合があります。<br><br>輝石には種類があり、入手できる輝石は<br>アドバンスクエストごとに異なります。<br><br>輝石はアークス・ロビーの輝石交換ショップで<br>エクスキューブや強力な武器と交換することができます。

text01 - Advance Quests are missions in which you will venture into and survey locations<br>with exceptionally strong enemies as part of the ARKS Advance Team.<br>Items called Advance Capsules are required to set out on an Advance Quest.<br>The payment of Advance Capsules will be conducted after touching the gate<br>in front of the Telepool.<br><br>It's not necessary for each player to have their own Advance Capsules, as<br>the party leader can also take responsibility for paying the way for each<br>member of the group.
text02 - Enemies slain on an Advance Quest may drop a type of item called <yellow>Astracite<c>.<br><br>There are various types of Astracite, and the type received differs based on<br>the current Advance Quest.<br><br>At the Astracite Exchange Shop in the ARKS Lobby, you can exchange Astracite<br>for a number of items, including EX-Cubes and powerful weaponry.
text03 - The weapons obtained through Astracite exchange have high base stats, as well as<br>other useful unique capabilities.<br><br>While you can make full use of these weapons as-is, you can also use them<br>as materials for weapon enhancement. Trade for more and more weapons<br>and continue to grow your character in strength!
text04 - When you complete an Advance Quest, that quest's AQ Threat Level will rise.<br><br>When its threat level reaches a certain point, the quest will receive<br>strengthened enemies and an overall increase in difficulty. Appropriately,<br>EXP and item drops will also be increased at a commensurate rate.<br><br>A quest's AQ Threat Level will reset to its default state after a fixed period<br>of time. You can check information on when it will reset at a Quest Counter.
