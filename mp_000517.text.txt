Beware, as Magatsu will <br>summon enemies, send parts of himself elsewhere, and use other <br>tricks to destroy Gates.
text0003 - Emergency Quick Trials (EQ Trials) are your ticket to countering <br>Magatsu's treachery. EQ Trials have a variety of goals, such as <br>evasion, interception, and eradication. Act quickly to meet the <br>demands of the situation. Successfully completing an EQ Trial<br>increases your quest result ranking and makes item drops <br>more frequent.
text0004 - Sukunahime's Protection is a power-up granted during the quest.<br>You can receive Sukunahime's Protection by damaging Magatsu, <br>destroying Magatsu's parts, and clearing EQ trials to accumulate points.<br>Level 1: 100 pts<br>Level 2: 500 pts<br>Level 3: 1000 pts
