text0001 - エネミーの部位破壊について説明します。<br><br>一部のエネミーには<c ffff00>部位破壊可能箇所<c>を<br>持っているものが存在します。

Title - Destroying Enemy Parts
text0001 - Certain enemies have specific body parts that can be destroyed in battle.
text0002 - For example, the horns and claws on the Snow Banser and Snow Banshee<br>you encounter while exploring the Tundra can be destroyed by dealing<br>enough damage to them.<br><br>Destroying enemy parts not only increases item drops,<br>but can also initiate special reactions from various bosses.
text0003 - For Snow Banser and Snow Banshee, by destroying the claws on all four<br>of their legs, they will react by slipping, providing a great opening to either<br>attack or heal, so you will want to be sure to destroy enemy parts<br>whenever you can.
