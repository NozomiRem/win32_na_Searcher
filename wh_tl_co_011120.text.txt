text01 - Attack power is divided into three categories:<br>Melee Power (MEL), Ranged Power (RNG) and Technique Power (TEC).<br><br>MEL impacts close-range attacks with melee weapons.<br>RNG impacts long-range attacks with ranged weapons.<br>TEC impacts all attacks with Techniques.<br><br>As a given stat increases, the strength of its respective form of attack will<br>gain in intensity.
text02 - Also, as Dexterity (DEX) increases, the range between an attack's maximum and<br>minimum damage output will narrow, allowing for more consistently-high<br>damage.<br><br>Defensive capabilities are also divided into three categories:<br>Melee Defense (Melee DEF), Ranged Defense (Ranged DEF) and<br>Technique Defense (Technique DEF).<br>Each category reduces incoming damage from its respective type.<br>Increasing DEX also lowers the amount of damage received.
