text01 - Characters and enemies may sometimes suffer from a status ailment after<br>taking a hit.

text03 - The icons for each status ailment are shown below.<br><br>　<pict ac_burn>: Burn<br>　<pict ac_frieze>: Freeze<br>　<pict ac_shock>: Shock<br>　<pict ac_mirage>: Mirage<br>　<pict ac_panic>: Panic<br>　<pict ac_poison>: Poison
text04 - Status ailments generally go away after some time has passed.<br>You can cure your and your allies' status ailments quickly with a Sol Atomizer.
