text01 - Just what ARE Skill Rings?<br>There are two kinds: L Rings, equipped on the left hand, and R Rings, equipped<br>on the right.<br>Equipping a Skill Ring gives you access to Ring Skills.<br><br>Example:<br><c 00ffff>L Ring Skill<c>: Katana Gear Guard Release<br>Automatically activates Katana Gear when you block for a certain amount of time.<br>While activated, the Gear Gauge fills more slowly.<br><br><c 00ffff>R Ring Skill<c>: PP Conversion/Ring<br>Temporarily increases automatic PP recovery rate and lowers max HP.
