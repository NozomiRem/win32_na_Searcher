text04 - 目的のアイテムを<br>入手するタイプの「マター」は<br>マターボード上で「ガイド」を<br>設定することで、対象クエストに<br>「ガイドマーク」が表示されます。

text00 - The Divergence Matrix
text01 - The Divergence Matrix is a system for progressing<br>through Episodes One through Three in the story.<br><br>A circular point is called a "Flux Point", and a<br>crucial gold-colored point is called a "Key Flux Point".<br><br>When you achieve various objectives set in these Flux Points, a new part of the<br>story unfolds.
text02 - Flux Point objectives include defeating enemies and<br>collecting items during quests.<br><br>Dropped target items will glow gold, so<br>be careful not to overlook them.
text03 - Episodes One and Two and Episode Three have Divergence Matrixes,<br>and you can progress through and complete all of them at the same time.<br><br>Switch between the episodes you want to progress<br>through from time to time.
text04 - There is a guide at the top of the Divergence Matrix<br>for Flux Points where you are to obtain target items.<br>Guide markers are displayed for those quests.
linewindow002 - <br>Select a Flux Point to investigate.<br>
