Title - Skill Ring Enhancement
text01 - Skill Rings can be enhanced up to their Enhancement Cap. <br><br>Earning EXP while a Skill Ring is equipped will also increase the Ring's EXP.
text02 - Max out a Ring's EXP to raise its Enhancement Cap.<br><br>Skill Rings can be enhanced at the Item Lab in the Shopping Plaza using meseta,<br>gathered materials, and other items.<br><br><yellow>Note: Skill Rings do not have to be enhanced to accumulate Ring EXP.
