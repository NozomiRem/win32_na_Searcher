text01 - The Photon Tree is a special plant shared by members of an alliance that<br>produces photons and is placed in their Alliance Quarters.<br><br>Players can gain a number of support effects from the plant's photonic power.
text02 - When a strong light is emitted by the Photon Tree, selecting<br>[Receive Tree Effect] will allow you to obtain a Photonic Effect from it.<br><br>Photonic Effects received will continue for a set period of time.<br>You cannot obtain more than one Photonic Effect at the same time.
text03 - The Photon Tree can be grown by using Alliance Points.<br>Growing the tree will enhance the effects it can bestow upon players.<br><br>Only the Alliance Leader and Officers can use points to grow the tree.
text04 - In order to obtain Photonic Effects from the Photon Tree, it must be watered<br>periodically with Photon Water. Without it, the tree will wither, and players<br>will no longer be able to gain Photonic Effects.<br><br>Giving the tree Photon Water will expend Alliance Points.
text05 - Collaborate with your fellow Alliance Members to complete lots of<br>Alliance Orders so your Photon Tree can grow proud and strong!
