text01 - サブクラスについて説明します。<br><br>サブクラスを設定すると、メインクラスを基本として<br>サブクラスのスキル・PA・テクニックが使えます。<br>また、サブクラスにも一部経験値が入ります。<br>（レベル上限があります）<br><br>サブクラスの設定はクラスカウンターの「クラス変更」から<br>「サブクラス選択」を選ぶことで行えます。

text01 - By setting a sub-class, you can use the skills, photon arts, and techniques<br>of that class while using your main class.<br>Some EXP gained will also go to the selected sub-class (up to the level cap).<br><br>You can set a sub-class by selecting "Change Class" and choosing<br>"Select Sub-Class" at the Class Counter.
text02 - By setting your main class to Hunter and your sub-class to Force,<br>you can expand on your options in battle, using a sword as your weapon<br>while retaining access to the techniques in your sub-palette.<br><br>However, you will be restricted to using the weapons and actions of your<br>main class.<br><br>This means that even with your sub-class set to Force, you will not be able to<br>equip Force-only rods.
